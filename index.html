About Ed

The Loop Never Stops

Edward Christopher Sheeran MBE is a British singer-songwriter who rose to global fame with his debut album + (Plus) in 2011. Known for his remarkable ability to loop guitar, vocals, and beatbox live on stage, Ed creates full layered soundscapes entirely on his own.

With albums like × (Multiply), ÷ (Divide), No.6 Collaborations Project, = (Equals), and Autumn Variations, Ed has sold over 150 million records worldwide, making him one of the best-selling music artists of all time.

The Loop Tour is his most ambitious live production yet — a celebration of the loop pedal technique that built his career, now reimagined at stadium scale across two continents.
